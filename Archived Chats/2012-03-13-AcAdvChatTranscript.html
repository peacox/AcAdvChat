Fifth Question
12:44:12AcAdvChat:MOD: Q5: If the student decides to return in the future, what is the process to re-enroll at your institution? #acadv
12:44:51gmcpmccord:Q3 We advise them based on their academic needs and encourage them to do it for themselves, not their parents. They're adults now. #AcAdv
12:45:01HowardSJ:@urbie Good point! Community in college is so different than in the "real" world! #acadv

12:47:50rgcox2820:Q5 Very easy - fill out new app. Everything else still on file. Biggest disadvantage is having to wait to register as new student #AcAdv

Final Thought
